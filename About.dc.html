Our Story

Schreüder Electrical was founded to bring genuine technical mastery to households and businesses across Cape Town and the surrounding areas. What began as a small team of artisan electricians has grown into a full-service provider spanning residential, commercial, and industrial work — without losing the personal attention every client deserves.

Every project is guided by the same principles: safety first, transparent pricing, and workmanship built to last. We stay current with regulatory standards so you never have to worry about compliance.
